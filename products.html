FAQ

Questions US Buyers Ask Us Most

How does sizing work? I don't want a box of returns.

All styles use Asian sizing — typically one to two sizes smaller than US equivalents. Every product has a centimeter-level size chart (chest, length, shoulder, sleeve), and most include recommended height/weight ranges. The easiest path: send us your team roster with heights and weights, and our team will map every person to the right size before you order. We also recommend ordering a sample size set first for programs over 50 pieces.

Can you add our company logo? What's the minimum?

Yes — embroidery and screen printing are done in our own workshops. Plain in-stock garments ship from one piece; for logo customization we'll quote based on quantity and stitch count, with minimums far below typical US decorators. Send us your vector logo (AI/EPS/PDF) for a free digital mockup before you commit.

What's the lead time to the US?

In-stock styles leave the warehouse in 2–5 business days. Express air (DHL/FedEx/UPS) typically delivers to US addresses in 5–10 days; economy and sea freight options are available for larger programs. Customized orders add embroidery/printing time, which we confirm with your quote.

How do I know the quality is what's pictured?

We encourage every new customer to order samples first — at single-piece prices. You'll see the YKK zippers, taped seams and fabric weights for yourself before placing a team order. Production runs exceed 10,000 pieces with per-batch QC, so your reorder next season matches what you approved this season.

How do I care for these garments?

Polos: machine wash cold, hang dry — the mercerized and liquid-ammonia treated cottons are specifically finished to resist shrinking, pilling and fading. Down and shell jackets: machine wash gentle or wipe clean; the Teflon™ and TPU-treated shells shrug off most stains with a damp cloth. Detailed care labels are sewn into every garment.
